Dive into all Game of Thrones books at Barnes & Noble. A Song of Ice and Fire is the riveting series that inspired HBO's hit TV show, Game of Thrones. The title of the HBO series, Game of Thrones, comes from the first book in the series. This epic fantasy book series is written by George R. R. Martin, an American novelist and short story writer dubbed "the American Tolkien" by Time magazine. If you haven't already watched or read the Game of Thrones series, the story takes place in a fictional world where seasons last for years and end unpredictably. The story chronicles around the power struggle between the great Houses of Westeros for the Iron Throne. No matter which Game of Thrones house is your favorite, reading the Game of Thrones books will take you on an adventure. Explore our wide selection of Game of Thrones books, hardcover box sets, paperbacks, eBooks, coloring books and more. These are the Game of Thrones books in order:

  • A Game of Thrones (A Song of Ice and Fire #1)
  • A Clash of Kings (A Song of Ice and Fire #2)
  • A Storm of Swords (A Song of Ice and Fire #3)
  • A Feast for Crows (A Song of Ice and Fire #4)
